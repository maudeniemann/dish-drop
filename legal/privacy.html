Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Dish Drop ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Dish Drop mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, display name, and password (stored securely hashed).
  • Profile Information: Optional bio and profile photo you choose to provide.
  • Content: Photos of dishes, ratings, reviews, and comments you post.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your approximate location to show nearby restaurants. You can disable this in your device settings.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type and operating system version for app compatibility.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Dish Drop service
  • To show you nearby restaurants and relevant content
  • To track meal donations and community impact
  • To enable social features (following, liking, commenting)
  • To send you notifications about activity on your posts (if enabled)
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and moderate content

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Other Users: Your public profile, posts, and activity are visible to other users.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and Vercel for API hosting. These providers process data on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen in the app, which will permanently remove your personal data.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data through your profile
  • Edit or update your information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Block other users
  • Report inappropriate content

Children's Privacy

Dish Drop is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and JWT-based authentication.

Tracking

Dish Drop does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers or participate in ad networks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@dishdrop.app.
