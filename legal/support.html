Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a post?

Tap the "+" button in the bottom navigation bar. Take a photo of your dish or select one from your library, add a rating, caption, and restaurant, then tap "Post".

How do meal donations work?

When you create a post, you can choose to donate a meal. Dish Drop partners with local food banks to convert your donations into real meals for people in need.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile tab → tap the Settings gear icon → scroll down to "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three-dot menu (⋯) on any post or profile and select "Report". You can also block users from the same menu.

How do I change my password?

Go to Profile → Settings → Change Password.

The app isn't loading / shows errors

Try closing and reopening the app. Make sure you have an internet connection. If the problem persists, email us at support@dishdrop.app.
